Processes and Communications

Our group will communicate via Microsoft Teams for one hour twice a week. Our scheduled meetings will be every Friday and Sunday at 8pm AEST. We also occasionally have unofficial meetings. If at any point in time we have questions to clarify or things to discuss with our group members, we communicate via the Microsoft Teams chat or via discord.

When a group member does not respond to communications by 8:20pm AEST, we start the meeting without them. Since all meetings are recorded, they are able to watch the recording though that is not ideal since their input is valuable during the meeting itself.
